About Me

Skills and Experience

I’m Kashif Ross, a web designer and developer in Las Vegas, Nevada that specializes in email marketing. I have two years of experience using HTML and CSS to create email, Craigslist, and social media campaigns. However, I consider myself a lifelong learner, so I regularly study backend technologies as well.

I have a wide range of skills that include photography and image editing in Photoshop and Lightroom. These skills have helped me to personally style websites and customize logos for real estate agents and DJs. Ultimately, I value professionalism and efficiency. Lastly, I pride myself in my research abilities and the effort I apply to all projects.

Personal Life

Additionally, I love traveling with my family and taking photos along the way. Over the past two years, I’ve visited Los Angeles, Texas, New York, Bangkok, Phuket, and Dubai. Last year, my team of four lived in Abu Dhabi. Now that we’re done teaching overseas, I aspire to return to programming full-time. My 2019 goals are to learn more and work harder than ever before. I hold myself accountable by setting weekly objectives.
